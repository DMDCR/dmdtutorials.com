about

Lifeline is a map-based safety app that helps users stay aware of nearby risks and reach help when needed. Lifeline offers live safety markers and community-sourced alerts. the app focuses on giving timely, location-based context while keeping user privacy central.

how to report a bug

best results: include app version, build id, device model, ios version, steps to reproduce, and screenshots or a screen recording. crash logs help a lot.

sample quick report
app: Lifeline
version: 1.2.3(4)
build: 2025.08.01
device: iPhone 16 Pro
ios: 26.0
summary: crash when tapping random button
steps: 1) open app 2) tap panic 3) select contact 4) crash
attach: screenshots, console logs, crash .ips

faq

is Lifeline free?
yes, the entire app is free. you can support me tho on Buy Me A Coffee via the app!
does Lifeline track me all the time?
depends, the app will use your location while in use for the map features and creating and publishing reports. the app will only use your location in small bursts in the background to check and send you accurate notifications. your location doesn't leave your device during this process.
is Lifeline open source?
no, not yet at least. Lifeline is proprietary software maintained by DMD. although some of our backend management stuff IS open source, check inside the app for more info.
